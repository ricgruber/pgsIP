Gianna Scatchell

Head of Litigation

Facebook Twitter Linkedin Email

Gianna understands the needs of professional, small business owners and start-ups, from her experience as attorney and starting several ventures outside of her legal career. Gianna's practice focuses on the intersection of law and technology focusing on privacy-based litigation including invasion of privacy claims sounding in cyber-bullying, defamation, and revenge porn.

Morever, Gianna counsels law enforcement agencies and schools addressing the unique challenges that technology causes in terms of privacy, compliance, online bullying, and criminal behavior. To continue to hone her skills and keep up with the rapid pace of technology, Gianna collaborates with the top executives from some of the world’s most successful start-ups through her continued involvement in Emerging Business Committee of Chicago and Chicago Summit Series Group.
