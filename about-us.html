About Us

We help our clients with a practice focused on IP. patents. startups. data. privacy. litigation.

Pasky Gruber Scatchell LLC was founded on the belief that intellectual property is essential and should be attainable for all businesses, regardless of size.

Pasky Gruber Scatchell LLC views each company in its entirety. By understanding your business goals and reviewing your proprietary interest, we can develop a comprehensive intellectual property asset management plan, enabling you and your company to achieve its vision.
