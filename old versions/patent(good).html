Practice Area

Patent

U.S. Patent Search & Patentability Assessment

A U.S. federal patent search is performed, searching only for granted U.S. patents and U.S. published patent applications. A patentability opinion is included in the fixed fee. Cost is dependent on complexity of invention.

U.S. Provisional Application for Patent

We draft and file one provisional application for a simple mechanical to a highly complex software or business method invention. This fee is exclusive of formal patent drawing costs (typically 150/sheet), and all government filing fees ($65-$260, depending on size of filing entity).

U.S. Utility Patent Application

We draft and file one utility patent application for a simple mechanical invention to a highly complex software or business method invention. This fee is exclusive of formal patent drawing costs (typically $150/sheet), and all government filing fees ($400-$1,600, depending on size of filing entity).
