Sean L. Harrington

Of Counsel

Linkedin Email CV

Sean is a cyber security professional, with seasoned expertise in digital forensics, information technology, regulatory compliance in the financial services sector, public policy, legislative drafting, risk management & governance, and legal ethics.

Sean's most rewarding and recent projects include providing cyber security legislative and policy contributions for several industry trade associations, contributing to the NIST Cybersecurity Framework through its workshops and RFIs, contributing a chapter on the code of professional ethics for the "Official (ISC)²® Guide to the Cyber Forensics Certified Professional CBK®," and providing legal and technical editing of white papers published in the William Mitchell Law Review as part of the Minnesota eDiscovery Working Group.

Sean has been a council member of the MSBA Computer & Technology Law Section for several years, chaired its legislative subcommittee and writes for its blog. He's also vice-chair of the cyber legislative working group of BITS/FSR (the Financial Services Roundtable), is a member of Infragard, FS-ISAC, the Cybersecurity working groups of SIFMA and the U.S. Chamber of Commerce, and is an advisory board member of Century College's Virtualization, Cloud, and Forensics program. In addition, Sean is a member of the 4th District Ethics Committee, serving Hennepin County, Minnesota.
