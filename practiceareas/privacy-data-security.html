PRIVACY, DATA SECURITY & INFORMATION LAW

Technology is now entrenched in every facet of businesss today. Our firm helps companies of all sizes manage the data risks associated with the collection, use and disclosure of electronically stored information, including sales, research, consumer and employee information. Our collaborative approach includes attorneys with experience in Intellectual Property, Litigation and Electronic Information Management practices.

☻ Develop privacy and information-security practices and policies tailored to your website, business, industry, and geographic region

☻ Information risk management assessment with privacy impact on organizational risk

☻ Determine and implement privacy safeguards: administrative, technical and physical

☻ Establish and educate organizations on privacy based upon information life cycle principles

☻ Privacy risk assessments: impact assessments, audit, and most importantly for startups-privacy by design™.
