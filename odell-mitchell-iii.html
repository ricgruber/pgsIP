Odell Mitchell III

Law Clerk

Facebook Twitter Linkedin Email

Odell earned his Bachelor of Music in Music Business from Millikin University in Decatur, IL. Originally from the Illinois suburbs of St. Louis, he moved to Chicago shortly after undergrad and resides in robust Bridgeport on Chicago’s south side.

Prior to law school, Odell worked at Apple, Inc. training local customers, regional employees, and corporate partners. He also founded his own creative consulting firm, Thirdinline Creative, (www.thirdinlinecreative.com) that creates designs for nationally recognized musicians and, in 2012, helped launch an album that debuted in iTunes Top 40 charts.

In law school, Odell focuses his studies on intellectual property, entertainment, and business law. He is the Vice President of the Student Bar Association, and he was selected to participate in 1871’s first-ever Campus 1871 (http://www.1871.com/a-recap-of-campus1871/)

As the law clerk, Odell works administratively with trademarks, patents, and copyrights, and he seeks to get more hands-on experience working with small businesses and start-ups. He is also active in the Chicago’s entrepreneurial and music scenes.
